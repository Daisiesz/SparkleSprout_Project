# 🌟 SparkleSprout Project

**An Educative, Life-Improving & Reflective Learning Platform for Kids (Ages 4–12)**



## 🎯 Purpose & Why It Exists

SparkleSprout is a **fun, colorful, and wholesome desktop application** designed to help children develop good habits, improve their learning, and reflect on their daily lives in a playful way.

It combines **productivity**, **math learning**, and **emotional wellness** into one easy-to-use hub — created especially for curious and growing kids in Kenya and beyond.

---

## ✨ What It Does

SparkleSprout brings together two powerful kid-friendly tools:

### 1. **Questly – Kids Adventure Planner** 🚀
- Interactive onboarding (name, age, interests)
- Monthly Big Dreams & Goals
- Daily Quests & Task Management
- Morning & Evening Journal
- Gratitude Practice (3 things they're thankful for)
- Points & Rewards System
- Focus Timer + Break Time Suggestions

### 2. **Kids Calculator** 🧮
- Large, colorful, easy-to-use interface
- Supports basic arithmetic + scientific functions (sin, cos, tan, etc.)
- Safe expression evaluation
- Calculation history
- Degree/Radian mode
- Perfect for young learners exploring math
